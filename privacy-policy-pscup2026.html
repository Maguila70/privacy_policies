Privacy Policy — PSCup2026

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This policy explains how the PSCup2026 application (the "App"), developed by Tatamobile (Gonzalo Astica), handles information when you use it. In short: the App does not require registration or a user account, does not collect personally identifiable information, and does not share your information with third parties for commercial purposes.

1. Information we do NOT collect

The App does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information. We do not create user profiles, we do not show advertising, and we do not include third-party analytics or tracking tools.

2. Information stored on your device

The App stores certain information only on your phone so that it works correctly:

  • Your preferences (for example, language and display settings).
  • The matches and teams you choose to follow.
  • Historical tournament data bundled within the App itself.

This information is stored locally (using the system storage and a local database on your device) and is not sent to any server of ours. If you uninstall the App, this data is removed.

3. Calendar permission

If you grant permission, the App can add match events to your calendar to help you remember them. This permission is used solely to create those events on your device. The App does not read, store, or transmit the contents of your calendar to any external server. You can revoke this permission at any time from your phone settings.

4. Notifications

With your permission, the App sends local notifications to alert you about goals and key plays for the matches or teams you follow. These notifications are generated on your device; you can disable them from your system settings.

5. Internet connection and match data

The App retrieves public match information (scores, schedule, and statistics) from the public ESPN API (site.api.espn.com). These requests do not include any personal information about you. Use of the data provided by ESPN is subject to that provider's terms.

6. Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

7. Security

Because information is kept on your device and we do not transmit personal data, the risk of exposure is minimal. We recommend keeping your device protected with your operating system's standard security measures.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or in legal requirements. The current version will be posted on this page, along with its update date.

9. Contact

For any questions about this policy or about how the App handles data, you can contact us at: support.pfma@gmail.com

PSCup2026 — Tatamobile. This policy applies to the version of the App distributed on Google Play and the App Store.
